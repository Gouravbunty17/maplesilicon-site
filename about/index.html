About Maple Silicon

Built by a GPU systems engineer for teams who pay real money for inference.

Maple Silicon Inc. is a Canadian company building runtime and compiler infrastructure for LLM inference on NVIDIA GPUs. We focus on one problem: cutting the cost of every token a production model serves, without forcing teams to retrain or change architectures.

Most inference optimization tools describe gains. We measure them. SparseFlow ships with a benchmark harness because the only number that matters is the one that holds up on your workload, on your hardware, in your serving stack.
