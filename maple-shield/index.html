System layers with a clear job to do.

An integrated stack that sees, tracks, scores, and explains — not a single component but the full sensing and operator layer.

Layer 01

Detection Engine

Computer vision on live video and edge sensor feeds — YOLOv8n tuned to surface airborne objects fast enough for real-time operator workflows.

Layer 02

Persistent Tracking

Stable track identities with IoU multi-object tracker, continuity across frames, and trajectory prediction to maintain operator trust under clutter and occlusion.

Layer 03

Alerting & Scoring

Risk-aware operator cues from CLEAR to CRITICAL — MQTT-streamed to C2 systems with STANAG 4586-compatible track messages.

Layer 04

Replay & Evidence

Incident history, replayable JSONL logs, and a structured audit trail that turns raw detections into post-event intelligence for operators and investigators.

Principles behind the product.

Lawful detection first. Edge deployment by default. A system that evolves without becoming a hardware-first trap.

01

Software-first moat

The durable value sits in perception, track continuity, and event intelligence — not custom hardware. The software stack is the product.

02

Edge-first runtime

Latency, resilience, and constrained deployment matter. Maple Shield runs close to the sensor — not only on a distant server.

03

Operator-grade interface

Understandable alerts, replayable evidence, and confidence that the workflow helps humans act — not overwhelm them.

How the workflow is meant to feel.

From sensor feed to trusted action — a chain that stays understandable at every step.

01

Sense

Ingest video or thermal feed, perform real-time detection, establish an initial track candidate with minimal delay.

02

Interpret

Stabilize the track, maintain identity, score against zone context, and filter alert noise before escalation.

03

Record

Log the event, preserve evidence, make the incident replayable for performance validation and post-event review.
