HyperGraphify

HyperGraphify converts hyper-edge events into graphlike chains so MWPM pipelines can run ‚Äî aiming to preserve probability structure rather than doing naive splitting.

Why MWPM breaks

Matching decoders operate on graphs: edges connect pairs of detectors. Hyper-edges represent multi-detector correlations that aren‚Äôt directly representable as a single graph edge.

  • You either lose MWPM or you risk incorrect decoding behavior
