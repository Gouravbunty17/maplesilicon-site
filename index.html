04 — How It Works

The Maple Shield
detection pipeline.

01

Sensor Fusion

RF signatures, acoustic profiles, and visual feeds are ingested simultaneously and time-synchronized on-device.

02

On-Device Inference

Optimized models run locally on SWaP-constrained hardware. No uplink required. No round-trip latency.

03

Threat Classification

Detections are scored, classified by drone type, and correlated across modalities to eliminate false positives.

04

Alert & Track

Operators receive real-time alerts with track history. All data stays local — no cloud, no exposure.
